Hi there.

My name is Jia, pronounciation: ja. Or if you'd like a challenge Jiashan Wu, pronounciation: jiāshān wú



I am a designer, technologist, and researcher. Currently I'm a graduate student in NYU Interactive Telecommunications Program and Partner at Dorm Room Fund. As a designer and art director I've worked with early stage startups, large enterprises, and nonprofits. I'm the co-founder of XOlator, a creative interactive agency that designs and build web platforms, applications, and data visualizations. As a Fulbright scholar, I've researched the design and manufacturing of Shanzhai phones in Shenzhen, China. I graduated from Parsons School of Design majoring in Communication Design.



These days I'm working with HTML/CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, microcontrollers such as Arduino and Flora, GSM modules, and lots of sensors.



On a clear day, you can find me biking in the friendly New York traffic and in Brooklyn, where I reside.

The Internet is about the now. Anything less than instant is a streaming #fail. In just twenty years, much of the Internet has been forgotten, over-written, or deleted. In various states of decaying, much of this content continues to exist. Just like a old box of photos or a journal from your teenage years, the archived Internet is waiting to be explored. Undo the effects of real-time by connecting to Wayback WiFi network to browse through time and data.



Wayback WiFi is created with the Wayback Machine in collaboration with Greg Leuch.
